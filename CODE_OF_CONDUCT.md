# 🤝 Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

AlgoViz was built by students, for students. We're committed to keeping this a space where everyone — regardless of experience level, background, or identity — feels welcome to learn, contribute, and grow.

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in AlgoViz a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

---

## Our Standards

### ✅ What we expect

- **Be kind and patient** — especially with beginners. Everyone starts somewhere.
- **Be constructive** — give feedback that helps, not hurts. Critique ideas, not people.
- **Be inclusive** — welcome different perspectives, backgrounds, and skill levels.
- **Assume good intent** — most people are trying their best.
- **Stay on topic** — keep discussions relevant to AlgoViz and its goals.
- **Give credit** — acknowledge others' work and ideas.

### ❌ What we won't tolerate

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Discriminatory jokes or language related to gender, race, religion, nationality, disability, age, or sexual orientation
- Unsolicited or off-topic sexual content
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or bad-faith engagement
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any behavior that makes contributors feel unwelcome or unsafe

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all AlgoViz spaces — GitHub Issues, Pull Requests, Discussions, commit messages, and any other project-related communication. It also applies when someone is representing AlgoViz publicly (e.g., at conferences, in blog posts, on social media).

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you witness or experience a violation, please report it by:

- **Emailing the maintainers** at: *(add your contact email here)*
- **Opening a private issue** if the behavior is not urgent

All reports will be handled confidentially. We will review every report and respond as quickly as possible.

### What Happens Next

Maintainers will investigate and may take one or more of the following actions, depending on the severity:

| Severity              | Action                           |
|-----------------------|----------------------------------|
| Minor / first offense | Private warning with explanation |
| Repeated or moderate  | Temporary ban from the project   |
| Severe or sustained   | Permanent ban                    |

We will not name reporters publicly without consent.
